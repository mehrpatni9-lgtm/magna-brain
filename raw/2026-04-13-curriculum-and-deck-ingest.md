## 10-Week Curriculum

**Format:** 1 weekly remote workshop × 10 weeks + 2-day in-person Magna graduation session
Each class: pre-work guided assessment + post-workshop applied exercise for their specific business

### Week 1: Foundations of Business and AI
Hyper-Learning introduction; business plan development with AI; overview of Generative AI and applications

### Week 2: Strategy and Innovation
Strategy and competitive advantage; innovation and creativity in AI applications; design thinking; technology trends and digital disruption

### Week 3: Leadership and Organizational Behavior
Leadership styles in the age of AI; team dynamics; decision making; managing change and resistance to AI adoption

### Week 4: AI Operations and Supply Chain Management
Operations management and process automation; supply chain optimization; quality control; sustainability and CSR

### Week 5: Financial Management
Financial analysis and valuation; capital budgeting for AI adoption; risk management; M&A in the AI industry

### Week 6: AI Marketing and Sales
Market research and consumer behavior; branding with AI; pricing strategies; digital marketing

### Week 7: Entrepreneurship and Venture Creation
Opportunity recognition; business planning; venture capital and funding; legal and regulatory issues

### Week 8: Global Business and International Strategy
Globalization and international business; cross-cultural negotiation; entry modes; political and economic risk analysis

### Week 9: Corporate Governance and Ethics
Corporate governance; ethics and CSR in AI; legal compliance; crisis and reputation management

### Week 10: Capstone Project
Final presentations; peer review; global trends wrap-up; Graduation ceremony
