## [pdf] MAGNA Masterclass Deck R1 — Build Your Founder Brand with AI — 14:30

A 33-slide masterclass deck + facilitator guide. Promise: *"Ninety minutes to the model. Fourteen days to the asset."* Build a founder brand using AI, structured on the MAGNA Model and Cialdini's science of influence.

### The MAGNA Model — five pillars, one compounding asset
`BRAND = IDENTITY × NARRATIVE × SYSTEM × REACH × REVENUE` — multiplicative, not additive. "One zero collapses the whole. Strength in one term can never rescue a zero in another." Governing equation: `CONSISTENCY × VALUE = COMPOUNDING`.

1. **Identity** — who you are before the performance begins. Core POV, values, voice. *Apply:* name your three non-negotiable beliefs; say them in every message.
2. **Narrative** — the story only you can tell; the throughline that frames everything you publish. *Apply:* anchor every post to one core story your audience can retell.
3. **System** — the engine that runs without you; repeatable process producing content without burnout. *Apply:* build the workflow once, let it run so output never depends on motivation. ("Inspiration is a guest. A system is a resident.")
4. **Reach** — how your message finds people who've never heard of you; channels + shareability. *Apply:* make every piece shareable so the audience distributes for you.
5. **Revenue** — where attention turns into income; offers + paths. *Apply:* name one clear offer your best content leads people toward.

Each pillar ships with an AI prompt ("Act as a brand strategist…", etc.).

### The Science of Influence — Cialdini's 7 principles
Frame: *"You are not posting. You are pre-suading."* **Pre-suasion** = the privileged moment before the message lands; "your brand is the soil, your content is the seed." The 7 forces:
1. **Reciprocity** — give first; people return the favor.
2. **Commitment & Consistency** — small yeses lead to larger ones.
3. **Social Proof** — in doubt, people follow others.
4. **Authority** — credentials + proof earn trust.
5. **Liking** — we say yes to those we relate to.
6. **Scarcity** — what is rare feels more valuable.
7. **Unity** — shared identity accelerates all.

### Pillars meet principles (the bridge)
- Identity + Proof → **Authority**
- Narrative → **Liking**
- System → **Commitment & Consistency**
- Reach + Value → **Reciprocity & Social Proof**
- Revenue + Community → **Scarcity & Unity**

### The 14-Day Sprint — "Two weeks. One living asset."
- **Week 1 (foundation + engine · Authority · Liking · Commitment):** Day 1 Niche & Purpose · Day 2 Identity & Visual · Day 3 Strategy & Calendar · Day 4 Signature Story · Day 5 Content & Repurpose · Day 6 Profiles & Hub · Day 7 Series & Lead Magnet.
- **Week 2 (distribute, monetize, compound · Reciprocity · Social Proof · Scarcity · Unity):** Day 8 Authority & Search · Day 9 Community & Collab · Day 10 Social & Video · Day 11 Email List · Day 12 Offers & Products · Day 13 Live & Income · Day 14 Measure & Plan 90.
- AI workflow: **Generate → Expand → Repurpose.** "AI drafts · You direct · The asset compounds."

### The Standard — three non-negotiables that never bend
1. **Consistency** — "a brand is a promise kept on a schedule." (Cialdini: Commitment)
2. **Value** — "give until it is uncomfortable." (Cialdini: Reciprocity)
3. **Voice** — "AI drafts. You decide. Anything that does not sound like you is a liability wearing your name." (Cialdini: Liking)
Closing: *"You will not finish with a perfect brand. You will finish with a living one. Perfect is a museum. Living is a market."*
