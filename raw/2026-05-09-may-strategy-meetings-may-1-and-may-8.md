## Cross-cutting themes (both meetings)

### 1. Positioning shift — academic → strategy firm

The biggest strategic decision of the two-week window. Magna repositions FROM "academic learning platform" TO "strategy firm helping founders scale revenue + find exits." Driven by Shailu's CEO feedback: entrepreneurs want help scaling and finding investors, not general learning. Affects website CTAs, content register, email templates, and positioning of the entire content engine.

### 2. Strategy consulting as the lead offering

Of all of Magna's offerings, *strategy consulting* shows the highest customer appetite. Mehr's content + email templates pivot to lead with this.

### 3. Personalization framework

Every post tags four-to-five dimensions: individual + company + industry + geography + area of interest. Bulk tagging is dead. Posts must benefit the tagged contact + Magna's outreach simultaneously.

### 4. The four-dimensional database

Industries × companies × cities × contacts — not as separate tables but as interlinked references. Click a company → see employees. Tag an industry → see all companies. Filter by city → see all contacts in geography. This is the structure underneath the Connect module.

### 5. Customer prioritization buckets

All customers triage into one of three: **Economic / Strategy / Capital.** Soman's prioritization task. Each bucket maps to a different engagement model.

### 6. The four-value capital delivery rule

Every Magna contract must deliver value in at least one of: Social / Emotional / Intellectual / Financial. This is the [[four-dimensions-of-wealth]] framework operationalized into a transactional test.

### 7. Cities priority for May–September

California + Dubai are the top two for May. 10–15 total key cities (including India). HBS Boston in August; India + Dubai conferences in September.

### 8. Founder Fridays (interview series)

NEW production model layered on top of the existing #FounderFridays content series: one founder interview per month, sourced via Zoom calls Mehr conducts. Initial candidates: **Sylvie, Heather Conrad, Ani**. Sylvie is first.

### 9. Content archive structure

Move from OneDrive → Google Drive. Archive content (pictures, documents, events) **by company**, mapped to city. This becomes the asset library for content production.

### 10. Magna directory state

109 community members total. 60 profiles complete. 28-of-94 referenced earlier (May 1) — list grew to 109 by May 8 with GLC attendees added. Top-100-meaningful-contacts is the curated layer.
