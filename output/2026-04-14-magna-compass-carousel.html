A map of every major industry in the world — organised into 3 simple categories.

For Mehr — Why We Made It This Way

What changed from the last version: The old carousel assumed people already knew what a sovereign wealth fund was, what "convergence themes" meant, and why 33 sectors mattered. This one assumes nothing. It starts from zero and builds up — like meeting someone new and telling them about Magna for the first time over coffee.

One idea per slide, nothing more. Slide 1: there's a map. Slide 2: most people don't have it. Slide 3: here's what it is. Slides 4–6: here are the three categories, one at a time. Slide 7: why it matters for your business. Slide 8: follow and engage. Each slide earns the next. Nothing is left unexplained.

Plain language throughout. "Sovereign wealth funds" is gone. "Core industries keep the world running" — anyone understands that. "Industries being built right now" — that's clearer than "transformative growth sectors." Same ideas, human words.

The closing question is specific and easy. "Which category — Core, Emerging, or Social?" gives people three clear options to choose from. Easy to answer. Gets real engagement because it's not "what do you think?" — it's a direct question with a simple answer format.

On length (8 slides): Went from 9 to 8. Removed the corridors and convergence deep-dives — those are future carousels in the series, not this introduction. The rule: the first post in a series earns the second one. This one's job is to make people curious, not to teach everything at once.
