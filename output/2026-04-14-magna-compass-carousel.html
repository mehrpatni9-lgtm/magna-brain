For Mehr — Why We Made It This Way

Why start with "Almost none have a world strategy"? Our founder audience is smart enough to know they have a business strategy. Telling them that isn't surprising. But "world strategy" is a different frame entirely — it's the one institutional investors use, and most founders have never been asked to think in those terms. That gap is what creates intrigue on slide 1 and makes them swipe.

Why show all 33 sectors? The sheer number of sectors is a credibility signal. When a founder sees that Magna maps everything from Defence to Synthetic Biology to Affordable Housing, they immediately understand this isn't a typical coaching program. It's a worldview. You can't fake 33 sectors — it either exists or it doesn't.

Why three different color palettes for the three pillars? Warm soil/gold for Core (stability, traditional wealth). Cool blue for Emerging (technology, future). Green for Social (growth, resilience, nature). The colors do the emotional work that the words describe. On Instagram, visual memory matters — the three pillars will feel different even before the text is read.

Why the convergence slide (slide 6)? This is the most sophisticated slide in the deck — and the most important. It shows that Magna's framework isn't about picking a sector, it's about seeing where forces intersect. That's the insight that separates institutional thinking from founder thinking. It's the "aha" moment of the whole carousel.

Why end with a question ("Which of the 33 sectors does your business intersect with?")? We never sell. But a question does something better than a CTA — it makes the reader think about themselves in relation to our framework. Every person who answers is telling us exactly where they are and what they need. And the comments become a list of exactly who follows us.

On length (9 slides): For an educational carousel on Instagram, 7–10 slides is the sweet spot. Under 6 feels thin. Over 10 and drop-off increases sharply. The sequence here builds like a story — hook, overview, three pillars, convergence, geography, founder application, close. Each slide earns the next one.
