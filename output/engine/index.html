The 9 content series

How Magna shows up every week

Nine series across Instagram and LinkedIn — each with its own voice, format, and rhythm. The engine that turns the brain into a posting cadence.

Series

#BehindTheBuild

LinkedIn Saturday (alternate weeks) text + photo post. A scene from inside the Magna ecosystem — a roundtable moment, a cohort exchange, an event recap. Simon Sinek register — community over audience.

Series

#CompassFramework

LinkedIn monthly document share. A complete, usable framework from the [[magna-compass]] — free and ungated. Magna's most authoritative content. Builds inbound from founders who want the application.

Series

#DigitalLectures

LinkedIn Saturday bi-weekly 8–10 slide carousel. Full business lecture, free and ungated. "Give the lecture away. Sell the application."

Series

#FounderFridays

Instagram Friday reel or story carousel. Real founder stories at inflection points. Simon Sinek register — human first, lesson second.

Series

#InspirationWeekends

Instagram Sunday single static. A reflective quote or principle that makes a founder think differently about the week ahead. Daniel Pink register — motivational but grounded.

Series

#MagnaMondays

Instagram Monday static. One sharp business truth per week. Naval Ravikant register. The post founders screenshot before 9am.

Series

#ThePrinciple

LinkedIn Tuesday long-form text. One operating truth per week, numbered, posted by [[shailu]]. Ray Dalio register. Builds the Magna canon over time.

Series

#TheSharpTake

LinkedIn Thursday short text post. A contrarian view on a widely-held business belief. Seth Godin register. Drives the most comments and shares of any series.

Series

#WisdomWednesdays

Instagram Wednesday 5–8 slide carousel. Brand-history case studies ending with a Magna framework takeaway. Seth Godin register.
