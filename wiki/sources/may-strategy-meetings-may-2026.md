# May Strategy Meetings — May 1 + May 8, 2026

Two consecutive Friday strategic planning sessions with Shailu, Mehr, Soman, Anil, Sireesha, and Raju. May 1 set the May architecture; May 8 refined content and locked the data model.

## What was decided

### Positioning

- Magna repositions FROM "academic learning platform" TO **strategy firm helping founders scale revenue and find exits**. Driver: Shailu's CEO feedback — entrepreneurs want scaling + investor help, not general learning.
- Website CTAs, content register, and email templates pivot accordingly.
- **Strategy consulting** is the lead offering — highest customer appetite of all Magna offerings.

### May projects (3)

1. **Digital outreach** — 5 companies/week from a 94-person prospect list, May 1–30. Target: 25 companies by May 30.
2. **Physical outreach** — Dubai and Chicago office visits. 5 Dubai companies scheduled in May.
3. **Website updates** — testimonial page refreshed mid-May.

### Content production

- 5–6 personalized posts by end of May, focused on prioritized cities and community members.
- "Founder Fridays" interview series — one founder per month. Initial candidates: Sylvie, Heather Conrad, Ani. Sylvie first; Shailu to facilitate intro.
- Less text-heavy, more image-based, tied to specific event photos.
- Personalization rule: every post tags individual + company + industry + geography + area of interest. No bulk tagging.

### Cities prioritization

- May focus: **California + Dubai**.
- Total key cities: 10–15 (Shailu to provide list).
- Travel: HBS Boston August; India + Dubai conferences September.

### Customer prioritization

- All customers bucketed into one of three: **Economic / Strategy / Capital**. Soman's prioritization task.
- Capital delivery rule: every contract must create value in at least one of **Social / Emotional / Intellectual / Financial** dimensions.
- Top-100 meaningful contacts > large-directory thinking.

### Data model (Connect module)

- Four-dimensional structure: **industries × companies × cities × contacts**, interlinked.
- Each company carries ≥1 industry tag from a predefined 33-industry list.
- Click company name → see all employees. Cross-sell mining built in.
- Single email per person. Duplicates cleaned. Magna IDs for everyone.
- Anil this month: Connect module + AI integration. Library next month.
- Two features releasing the week of May 1: meeting-notes summarization + educator/conscious-power-user functionality.

### Content archive

- Move from OneDrive → Google Drive.
- Archive **by company**, mapped to city.

### Community channel

- Magna WhatsApp channel by June 1.
- All customer interactions documented in Connect module.

### Testimonials
