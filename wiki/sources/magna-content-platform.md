## What this file is

A self-contained HTML document titled **"MAGNA — Content Platform 2026"** that lays out Magna's full content strategy: 9 content series across 4 platforms, bound by the 6-voice Tone Compass. The single most load-bearing document in `reference/`.

## Structure (as extracted)

### 4 platforms
- **Instagram** — 4 posts/month, 2–3 reels/week, daily stories. Builds emotional resonance through ritual recognition.
- **LinkedIn** — Shailu's personal brand, 2–3 posts/week. Thought leadership, not marketing.
- **YouTube** — long-form lectures. "The lecture hall goes digital."
- **Twitter/X** — compressed mirrors of IG/LI content. 1/day, 5 days/week.
