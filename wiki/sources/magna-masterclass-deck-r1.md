Faithful extraction of Shailu Tipparaju's 33-slide masterclass deck (+ facilitator guide), "Build Your Founder Brand with AI." Promise: *"Ninety minutes to the model. Fourteen days to the asset."*

## The MAGNA Model — five pillars

`BRAND = IDENTITY × NARRATIVE × SYSTEM × REACH × REVENUE` (multiplicative — one zero collapses the whole). Governing law: `CONSISTENCY × VALUE = COMPOUNDING`.

1. **Identity** — who you are before the performance begins; core POV, values, voice. Apply: name three non-negotiable beliefs, repeat in every message.
2. **Narrative** — the story only you can tell; the throughline. Apply: anchor every post to one retellable story.
3. **System** — the engine that runs without you. Apply: build the workflow once; "inspiration is a guest, a system is a resident."
4. **Reach** — how the message finds strangers; shareability. Apply: make every piece shareable.
5. **Revenue** — where attention becomes income. Apply: name one clear offer your content leads toward.

Each pillar carries a ready AI prompt.

## The Science of Influence — Cialdini's 7 principles

Frame: *"You are not posting. You are pre-suading."* **Pre-suasion** = the moment before the message lands; "your brand is the soil, your content is the seed."

Reciprocity · Commitment & Consistency · Social Proof · Authority · Liking · Scarcity · Unity.

**Pillars → principles:** Identity+Proof → Authority · Narrative → Liking · System → Commitment · Reach+Value → Reciprocity & Social Proof · Revenue+Community → Scarcity & Unity.

## The 14-Day Sprint

- **Week 1** (Authority · Liking · Commitment): Niche & Purpose · Identity & Visual · Strategy & Calendar · Signature Story · Content & Repurpose · Profiles & Hub · Series & Lead Magnet.
- **Week 2** (Reciprocity · Social Proof · Scarcity · Unity): Authority & Search · Community & Collab · Social & Video · Email List · Offers & Products · Live & Income · Measure & Plan 90.
- AI workflow: **Generate → Expand → Repurpose.** "AI drafts · You direct · The asset compounds."

## The Standard — three non-negotiables

1. **Consistency** — "a brand is a promise kept on a schedule." (Commitment)
2. **Value** — "give until it is uncomfortable." (Reciprocity)
3. **Voice** — "AI drafts. You decide. Anything that does not sound like you is a liability wearing your name." (Liking)

"Perfect is a museum. Living is a market."
