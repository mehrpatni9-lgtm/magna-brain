## Typography

- **Primary display:** Forum Serif
- **Secondary:** Aether (referenced in brand guide; exact usage unspecified in extracted text)
- **Body fallback:** Raleway (used in [[magna-content-platform]] as the body font)

## Colour palette

| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Charcoal | `#2D2D2D` | Background — depth and seriousness |
| Creme | `#E8DFC8` | Primary typography — warmth and clarity |
| Soil Brown | `#5C3518` | Accent — thoughtful, restrained |
| Taupe | `#483C32` | Accent — restrained, grounded |

## Brand direction

> "A grounded, intellectual palette that feels authoritative yet human."
>
> Charcoal background for depth and seriousness.
> Cream and brown typography for warmth and clarity.
> Taupe and soil tones as thoughtful, restrained accents.
> Aether & Forum Serif for timeless, academic strength.
>
> **"Quiet confidence. Depth of thought. Enduring credibility."**

## Usage notes for content

- Every [[output]] HTML draft should embed this palette inline so Mehr sees a brand-consistent preview immediately.
- No other colours. No gradients unless specified in a future brand update.
- Serif is the display face (headings). Sans is the body face.
