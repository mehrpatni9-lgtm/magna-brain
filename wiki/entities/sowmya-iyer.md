**One-line definition:** Pune-born, Dubai-based founder-CEO of DViO Digital; 20+ years in digital marketing; built and bought back her own agency; Harvard OPM 63 graduate; TEDx speaker; next Founder Friday slot (June 2026).

## Key facts

### Current

- **Founder & CEO, DViO Digital** — 130+ team, 6 markets (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Dubai, Africa/SE Asia), 3 countries. [[central-info-archive-may-2026]]
- Based in Dubai (splits time with Mumbai). [[central-info-archive-may-2026]]
- **DViO One** — AI-powered enterprise SaaS platform for media, marketing, and sales operations. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- **LEAP** — AI-driven data and digital transformation company. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- **Chief Mentor, MAD-Tech Incubator** — supports Ad-Tech, MarTech, Web3, and Experiential Tech startups. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- **Advisor to Government of India** on digital marketing strategy. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- Keynote speaker: IAMAI, FICCI, Ministry of Commerce, Harvard HBS (OPM 64 cohort). [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]

### Prior career

- **Founded Xebec Digital (XD), 2007** — started as a digital division of a mainline agency; scaled to one of India's largest independent digital agencies. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- **Bought out all partners, 2017** — acquired 100% ownership and rebranded as DViO Digital. High-conviction, high-risk move. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- **Research Executive, Indica Research (2005–06)** — data-first thinking forged here; clients: Hutch, Toon Disney, Whirlpool. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- **Content Developer, Harbinger Knowledge Products (2002–04)** — instructional design for US and Singapore markets. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- **Age 17 (2002):** launched 15to25.com — a youth edutainment web portal — before India had mainstream internet. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]

### Education

- **Harvard Business School — OPM 63 (2023–2025).** [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- **PG Diploma in Communication Management — Indira School of Communication, Pune.** [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- **B.A. English Literature — Symbiosis College, Pune.** [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]

### Personal

- Born Pune, Maharashtra. Tamil Brahmin upbringing. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- Morning rituals: journaling, meditation, South Indian filter coffee — credits these as foundation of creative capacity. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]
- Married to Shrikant Pattathil (President, Harbinger Systems Inc.). One daughter, Aarini. [[sowmya-iyer-bio-dossier]]

## In her own words (Founder Fridays interview, June 2026)

- **Pull vs push** — "marketing that doesn't look like marketing" = brands people come to vs. brands that push themselves in your face and breed skeptics. [[sowmya-iyer-founder-friday-interview]]
- **On AI** — "the last 18 months have made my last 13 years feel slow"; she's prepared while others are in a "we need to catch up" rut. [[sowmya-iyer-founder-friday-interview]]
- **Systems, not services** — traditional agencies bill hours and ignore outcomes; DViO is a "growth operating system." [[sowmya-iyer-founder-friday-interview]]
- **The buyout, in her words** — "I had to mortgage the house I lived in"; every consultant said no; she trusted the "entrepreneur's gut." [[sowmya-iyer-founder-friday-interview]]
- **Leadership** — quotes Simon Sinek's "leaders eat last"; her own line: "one leg in the future, one leg in the present." [[sowmya-iyer-founder-friday-interview]]
- **OPM rationale** — entrepreneurs become "a frog in the well"; "increase your surface area for opportunities to come." [[sowmya-iyer-founder-friday-interview]]
- **On gender** — self-funded, never raised capital, never faced discrimination (incl. an all-male Sharia-compliant board still on her roster); "women tend to identify so much with their own gender that they ruin it for themselves." Controversial; has taken public backlash. [[sowmya-iyer-founder-friday-interview]]
- **Superpower wish** — to be in multiple places at once. [[sowmya-iyer-founder-friday-interview]]
- **This or That** — Boardroom · work-life integration (freedom) · Solo founder · Client dinner. [[sowmya-iyer-founder-friday-interview]]
