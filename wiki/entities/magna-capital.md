**One-line:** Magna's investment arm — early-stage capital deployed into startups aligned with the Compass thesis, with a distinctive requirement that every entrepreneur and partner first complete the Magna curriculum.

## Key facts

- **Investment thesis (verbatim)**: *merit · market · maturity*. [[magna-master-enrichment]]
- Focus: startups solving real-world problems, leveraging emerging technologies, disrupting traditional industries. [[magna-vip-site]]
- Sustainability, social responsibility, ethical practices are explicit screens. [[magna-vip-site]]
- **Unique requirement**: every entrepreneur, partner, or investor in Magna Capital must go through the Magna curriculum first. Capital and education are intentionally linked. [[magna-master-enrichment]]
- Stated purpose (verbatim): *"entrepreneur empowerment, visionary investment strategies, partnership and collaboration, long-term value creation, impact and purpose."* [[magna-vip-site]]

## Confirmed portfolio companies (5)

- [[careerx]] — cohort EdTech, Hyderabad. Trains + places students in top tech companies.
- [[student-tribe]] — India's largest student community platform. Founded by Sri Charan Lakkaraju.
- [[shiminly]] — K-12 holistic cross-disciplinary programs.
- [[muffins]] — Dubai-based remote tech teams. Track record of producing unicorns and acquisitions.
- [[docsynk]] — Healthcare AI, Dallas. Bridge funding from Colosseum Feb 2020 (their 9th global investment at the time).

All five align with Magna's Social pillar (Education & Human Capital) or the AI-Enabled Healthcare convergence theme — confirming Magna Capital invests in exactly what the Compass recommends. See [[magna-invests-in-its-own-thesis]].
