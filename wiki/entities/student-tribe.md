**One-line:** India's largest student community platform — *"students' LinkedIn"* — connecting Tier 2/3 college students with companies, brands, and career opportunities. Formerly STUMAGZ. A [[magna-capital]] portfolio company.

## Key facts

- **HQ**: Hyderabad, India. [[magna-portfolio-companies]]
- **Founder**: Sri Charan Lakkaraju — Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia 2018; received a Richard Branson shoutout. [[magna-portfolio-companies]]
- **Scale**: 500,000+ students; 200+ influencers; ₹12 lakh+ income generated for students; 55+ full-time placements. [[magna-portfolio-companies]]
- **Stated philosophy**: *"Talent and skill triumph over the prestige of your alma mater."* — directly aligned with Magna's belief in democratising access to excellence. [[magna-portfolio-companies]]
- **Compass alignment**: Education & Human Capital (Social pillar).
