**One-line:** A practitioner-led strategy and learning brand for founders scaling through the $10M–$50M wall, built on 25 years of operating experience.

## Key facts

- Founder-led by Shailu (see [[shailu]]). LinkedIn is Shailu's personal brand; Instagram is the Magna brand [[magna-content-platform]].
- Framework stack is called the **MAGNA Compass** — five pillars (strategy, negotiation, capital, culture, leadership) spanning 33 sectors [[magna-compass]].
- Programs include [[quantum-leap]] (founder cohorts), [[magna-academy]] (education), and [[dubai-roundtables]] (events).
- Geo anchor: Dubai. Global reach [[magna-content-platform]].
- Visual identity: charcoal (#2D2D2D) + creme (#E8DFC8) + soil brown (#5C3518) + taupe (#483C32). Forum Serif + Raleway typography. "A grounded, intellectual palette that feels authoritative yet human." [[magna-brand-guide]]
- Brand essence line: "Quiet confidence. Depth of thought. Enduring credibility." [[magna-brand-guide]]
- Commercial model: free lectures and frameworks, paid application. "Give the lecture away. Sell the application." [[magna-content-platform]]
