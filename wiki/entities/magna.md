**One-line:** A global intelligence and education firm that translates institutional-grade sector knowledge into founder and executive capability — so the people building the next generation of businesses see the world the way the world's largest investors see it.

## Key facts

### What Magna actually is (corrected 2026-04-13)

- Magna is a **global strategic research and advisory firm** providing **institutional-grade sector intelligence** to sovereign wealth funds, family offices, private equity, and multinational corporates. The education product is downstream of the intelligence product, not its core. [[magna-compass-r3]]
- Tagline (institutional): *"Delivering clarity where complexity meets capital allocation."* [[magna-compass-r3]]
- Tagline (platform): *"Multi-lingual, global, executive community platform to Learn · Connect · Collaborate."* [[magna-master-enrichment]]
- Parent organisation: [[colosseum-group]] (Chicago) — Magna sits under it alongside Colosseum Academy, Foundation, Labs, LaunchPad, Partners, Studios, Ventures. [[magna-vip-site]]
- Founder-led by [[shailu]]. LinkedIn is Shailu's personal brand; Instagram is the Magna brand. [[magna-content-platform]]

### The three audiences Magna serves

1. **Founders and executives ($10M–$50M+ growth stage)** — primary education audience; [[magna-academy]] is the entry point. [[magna-master-enrichment]]
2. **Institutional investors** (SWFs, family offices, PE, multinationals) — served via [[magna-compass]] — the 33-sector global industry blueprint. [[magna-compass-r3]]
3. **Early-stage startups** — served via [[magna-capital]]; investment thesis is *merit · market · maturity*. Every founder/investor must complete the Magna curriculum first. [[magna-master-enrichment]]

### The intellectual stack

- **The Compass** — 33 sectors · 3 investment pillars · 4 geographic dimensions · 6 frontier corridors · 7 nations profiled. [[magna-compass]] [[magna-compass-r3]]
- **The 3 C's (solution-level)**: Compass · Connections · Collaborations. [[magna-3cs]] [[magna-curriculum-r4]]
- **The 3 lifecycle phases**: Creation · Acceleration · Transition. [[creation-acceleration-transition]]
- **Hyper-Learning Framework**: Participation · Distillation · Application — Magna's proprietary pedagogy. [[hyper-learning-framework]]
- **ABCD Framework**: Awareness · Balance · Creativity · Design — leadership development model. [[abcd-leadership-framework]]
- **"Quantum of Greatness ®"** — the program name and philosophy. [[quantum-of-greatness]]
- **"Think in Ink ®"** — proprietary reflection framework ("inking the thinking"). [[think-in-ink]]

### Programs

- [[magna-academy]] — 10-week applied-MBA cohort (Quantum of Greatness ® certification).
- [[magna-meetup]] — 3-day intensive intensive perspective-shift format.
- [[magna-capital]] — investment arm.
- [[quantum-leap]] — startup accelerator-style growth program (Startup Hyderabad partnership confirmed). [[magna-vip-site]]
- [[dubai-roundtables]] — invite-only founder roundtables; 40 founders over 3 years; sessions not recorded. [[magna-master-enrichment]]
- **VIP membership tiers**: Growth (1 workshop/month) · Scale (2/month + introductions + concierge) · Enterprise (3/month + PR/Brand/Publishing + dedicated concierge).

### Market context

- E-learning market: $374.3B by 2026 (14.6% CAGR). [[magna-curriculum-r4]]
- Executive education: $39B → $109.8B by 2031 (11.2% CAGR). [[magna-curriculum-r4]]
- No single competitor has >5% market share. [[magna-curriculum-r4]]

### Competitive landscape

- **Direct**: OnDeck, Torch, BetterUp, Singularity University.
- **Magna's differentiation**: only platform combining institutional-grade sector intelligence (the Compass) with executive education. Deep educator access (Ivy League + practitioners). Broad network (YPO, IVY, IEEE, WEF, UN, VC firms). Wide experience (Shailu's 4 EdTech exits, 100+ companies advised).
