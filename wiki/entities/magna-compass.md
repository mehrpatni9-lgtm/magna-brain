**One-line:** Magna's institutional-grade global industry blueprint — 33 sectors, 3 investment pillars, 4 geographic dimensions, 6 frontier corridors. The intellectual spine that unifies Magna's intelligence, education, and capital products.

## Calibration note (2026-04-13)

The original brain entry said the Compass was a "five-pillar framework: strategy, negotiation, capital, culture, leadership — 33 sectors." That conflated two distinct frameworks:

- **The Magna Compass (THIS entity)** = the institutional product — a 33-sector global industry blueprint with 3 investment pillars (Core / Emerging / Social), 4 geographic dimensions, and 6 frontier corridors. Used by SWFs, family offices, PE, multinationals.
- **The 5 content pillars** (strategy, negotiation, capital, culture, leadership) = how Magna organises its **content** for founders. They live as concept files: [[magna-pillar-strategy]], [[magna-pillar-negotiation]], [[magna-pillar-capital]], [[magna-pillar-culture]], [[magna-pillar-leadership]]. They are NOT the Compass.

Both coexist. This entity tracks the Compass-the-institutional-product. The 5 content pillars stay where they are. [[magna-compass-r3]] [[magna-master-enrichment]]

Sector leadership by nation: USA · China · Germany · Japan · India · Saudi Arabia · Singapore. Resource endowments (natural, human, institutional, infrastructure capital).

### The 6 Frontier Growth Corridors

1. **Indo-Pacific Digital Belt** (India, Singapore, South Korea, Japan) — $3T+ GDP corridor.
2. **Gulf Transformation Zone** (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar) — $1T+ SWF redeployment.
3. **African Continental Corridor** (Nigeria, Kenya, Egypt, South Africa) — youth demographic.
4. **Latin American Resource Belt** (Brazil, Chile, Peru, Colombia) — critical minerals.
5. **European Resilience Cluster** (Germany, France, Poland, Nordics) — industrial re-armament.
6. **Central Asian New Silk Road** (Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Azerbaijan) — transit corridor.

### The 4 Strategic Convergence Themes (highest-conviction theses)

1. **Energy Transition Stack** — Core energy + Emerging climate tech + Social access.
2. **AI-Enabled Healthcare** — Core healthcare + Emerging AI/biotech + Social systems.
3. **Human Capital Economy** — Core professional services + Emerging XR/EdTech + Social education.
4. **Geopolitical Resilience** — Core defence + Emerging quantum/semiconductors + Social food security.
