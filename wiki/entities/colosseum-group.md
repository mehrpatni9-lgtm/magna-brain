**One-line:** Chicago-based global enterprise that sits above [[magna]] — Shailu's founding holding company across venture capital, education, creative branding, and strategic communications.

## Key facts

- Founded and chaired by [[shailu]]. [[magna-vip-site]]
- HQ: Chicago, IL. [[magna-vip-site]]
- Magna sits as one of Colosseum Group's businesses — alongside the divisions below.

### Seven divisions

1. **Colosseum Academy** — education.
2. **Colosseum Foundation** — giving back.
3. **Colosseum Labs** — R&D / innovation.
4. **Colosseum LaunchPad** — incubation.
5. **Colosseum Partners** — advisory.
6. **Colosseum Studios** — creative / design.
7. **Colosseum Ventures** — investments. (Provided Feb 2020 bridge funding to [[docsynk]] as Colosseum's 9th global investment.) [[magna-portfolio-companies]]
