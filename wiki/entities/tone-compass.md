**One-line:** Magna's voice system — six reference voices (Naval, Dalio, Sinek, Godin, Pink, Robbins) mapped to specific content series.

## The six voices

1. **Naval Ravikant** → [[content-series-magna-mondays]]. Dense, compressed, one big idea, no fluff.
2. **Ray Dalio** → [[content-series-the-principle]], [[content-series-digital-lectures]]. Numbered principles, radical transparency, pattern recognition.
3. **Seth Godin** → [[content-series-the-sharp-take]], [[content-series-wisdom-wednesdays]]. Provocative but never loud. Two-sentence observation that rewires how you see the market.
4. **Simon Sinek** → [[content-series-founder-fridays]], [[content-series-behind-the-build]]. Human before lesson. Why before what.
5. **Daniel Pink** → [[content-series-inspiration-weekends]]. Motivational but grounded in research. Autonomy. Mastery. Purpose.
6. **Tony Robbins** → CTAs & event posts only. Energy and momentum, restrained. **Never the default.**

## Usage rule

Voice is chosen by **content series**, not by mood. If a post doesn't fit any existing series, either (a) pick the closest series and assign that voice, or (b) file a `brain-architecture` feedback issue asking whether a new series is needed.
