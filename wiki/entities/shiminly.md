## Key facts

- **What they do**: cross-disciplinary programs for K-12 schools and private students, framed as *education-as-self-discovery*. [[magna-portfolio-companies]]

- **Why it matters for Magna**: Shiminly is **Magna's thesis applied at the K-12 level**. The same belief that runs through Magna Academy (founders develop personally as they build) runs through Shiminly (students develop personally as they learn).
