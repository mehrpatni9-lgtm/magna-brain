## Cluster 4 — Non-negotiable tone rules

- Quiet confidence over loud conviction. Magna states; it does not shout.
- First line must be screenshot-worthy — no warm-ups.
- Specific over abstract (real numbers, real cities, real scenes).
- Never CTA-as-close. Every post ends on a question or a shareable truth.
- No hustle culture, no buzzwords, no motivational poster content.
- Every post ties back to a worldview thesis in [[voice/styleguide]].
