**Post:**

Networking events don't build networks. Shared problems do.

The best professional relationships I have came from being in the same room when something went wrong — not when everyone was performing.

**Why it's an exemplar:** Distinguishes performance from real connection. First-person authority ("I have") without humblebrag. Short enough to screenshot.
