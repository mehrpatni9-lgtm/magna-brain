**Hook:** The business you're building will outlast the version of you that started it. Build accordingly.

**Caption:**

The founder who starts a company is never the same person who scales it. Or exits it.

Each phase demands a different version of you.

The question isn't how to build a bigger business. It's whether you're becoming the person that business actually needs.

**Why it's an exemplar:** Daniel Pink register — motivational but grounded in the founder's actual developmental arc. Reflective, not rah-rah. Sunday-appropriate.
