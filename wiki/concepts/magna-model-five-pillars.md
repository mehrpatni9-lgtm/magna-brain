**TL;DR:** A founder brand is built on five pillars — **Identity · Narrative · System · Reach · Revenue** — and they *multiply*, not add: `BRAND = IDENTITY × NARRATIVE × SYSTEM × REACH × REVENUE`. One weak term (a zero) collapses the whole; strength in one can never rescue a zero in another. The compounding law beneath it: `CONSISTENCY × VALUE = COMPOUNDING`. [[magna-masterclass-deck-r1]]

## Why it matters for Magna

This is the spine of Shailu's "Build Your Founder Brand with AI" masterclass — the model promised in "ninety minutes." It's a clean, teachable asset that maps directly onto Magna's [[positioning-shift-strategy-firm]] (founders want to *build something that scales*, not learn in the abstract) and onto the [[content-series-founder-fridays]] / personal-brand content engine. It also gives the community a shared vocabulary for diagnosing a stalled brand: find the zero.

## The five pillars

1. **Identity** — who you are before the performance begins; core POV, values, voice. *Apply:* name three non-negotiable beliefs and repeat them everywhere.
2. **Narrative** — the story only you can tell; the throughline that frames everything you publish. *Apply:* anchor every post to one retellable story.
3. **System** — the engine that runs without you. *Apply:* build the workflow once — "inspiration is a guest, a system is a resident."
4. **Reach** — how the message reaches strangers; channels + shareability. *Apply:* make every piece shareable so the audience distributes it.
5. **Revenue** — where attention turns into income. *Apply:* name one clear offer your best content leads people toward.
