**TL;DR:** LinkedIn monthly document share. A complete, usable framework from the [[magna-compass]] — free and ungated. Magna's most authoritative content. Builds inbound from founders who want the application.

## Format

- **Platform:** LinkedIn document share (PDF/slide deck)
- **Cadence:** Monthly
- **Voice:** Ray Dalio — authoritative, framework-first
- **Structure:** Title → premise → framework components → application example → one-line takeaway
- **Length:** Document — typically 10–20 pages

## Ground rules

- **One framework per release.** Full, not excerpted.
- **Free and ungated.** No email capture.
- **Pillar rotation.** Alternates across the five [[magna-compass]] pillars so the cadence covers one pillar every ~5 months.
- **"Give the framework away. Sell the application."** The monthly release is the most senior expression of this rule.

## Relationship to other series

- [[content-series-digital-lectures]] — bi-weekly carousels are shorter expressions of frameworks drawn from this series
- [[content-series-the-principle]] — individual principles from each framework become #ThePrinciple posts between releases
