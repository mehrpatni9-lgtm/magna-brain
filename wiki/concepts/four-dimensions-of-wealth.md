**TL;DR:** [[shailu]]'s framework for true wealth and true leadership. Wealth has four dimensions — Social, Emotional, Intellectual, Financial — and most people optimise one or two and wonder why they feel unfulfilled despite success.

## Why it matters for Magna

This is the load-bearing framework underneath [[shailosophy]] and a recurring substrate in Shailu's writing. It anchors any post about wealth, success, leadership, or fulfilment in something other than money. It is also the explicit rebuttal to single-axis success narratives that dominate founder content.

The "quantum" lens (see [[quantum-worldview]]) is what makes this framework non-trivial: the four dimensions don't add up — they entangle. A change in one shifts the others.

## The four dimensions

| Dimension | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Social** | Relationships, community, network, trust capital. |
| **Emotional** | Resilience, empathy, emotional intelligence, inner life. |
| **Intellectual** | Ideas, curiosity, learning, frameworks, insight. |
| **Financial** | Money, enterprise value, capital allocation. Money is one *output* of the above, not the goal. |

## Verbatim Shailu

> *"Society has conditioned us to view wealth through a singular lens — money. Yet, true wealth transcends numbers in a bank account."* [[shailu-ideology-deep-read]]
