**TL;DR:** The first pillar of [[magna-compass]]. Strategy as clarity on which problem is actually the problem — not as a framework you buy.

## Why it matters for Magna

The strategy pillar is the explicit anti-thesis of generic business content. Magna's position: most founders don't have a strategy problem — they have a *thinking* problem. Strategy, as Magna teaches it, is the work of identifying which problem is load-bearing. Everything else is optimisation around the wrong thing.
