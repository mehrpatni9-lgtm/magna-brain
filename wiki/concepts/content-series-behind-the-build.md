**TL;DR:** LinkedIn Saturday (alternate weeks) text + photo post. A scene from inside the Magna ecosystem — a roundtable moment, a cohort exchange, an event recap. Simon Sinek register — community over audience.

## Format

- **Platform:** LinkedIn text + photo
- **Cadence:** Alternate Saturdays (offset from [[content-series-digital-lectures]])
- **Voice:** Simon Sinek — the why behind the room
- **Structure:** A scene → what it revealed → the implicit "this is what Magna is"
- **Ending:** Often a quiet community claim, not a CTA

## Signature phrases from `reference/`

- *"We don't run events. We build rooms where real problems get solved."*
- *"The session wasn't recorded. But the shift in the room was real."*
- *"3 years ago MAGNA didn't exist. This week we hosted our 40th founder."*

## Ground rules

- **Show the community, don't promote it.** The post reveals the quality of the room through a specific moment, not through headcount or testimonials.
- **The photo is part of the post.** Never a stock image. Never a logo.
- **No selling.** No "apply now" links.
