**TL;DR:** Magna's leadership development model — four dimensions a leader is asked to elevate simultaneously: **Awareness · Balance · Creativity · Design**. Distinct from [[hyper-learning-framework]] (which is *how* learning happens) and from [[four-dimensions-of-wealth]] (which is Shailosophy's life-frame).

## Why it matters for Magna

ABCD is the leadership-side scaffolding inside the [[magna-academy]] curriculum. It gives Magna a clear answer to *"what does a Magna-trained leader become better at?"* — not a vague "they grow," but specifically: more aware, more balanced, more creative, more design-minded.

## The four dimensions

- **A — Awareness.** Of self, of others, of the system the leader operates in.
- **B — Balance.** Across the [[four-dimensions-of-wealth]]; across the company's internal portfolio; across the founder's personal/professional/social goals.
- **C — Creativity.** Generative capacity — for problems, products, frameworks, conversations.
- **D — Design.** Intentional construction — of teams, of customer experience, of strategic moves.

## Magna's framing

> The ABCD framework is described as unlocking *"the quantum of elements"* — an extensive compass toolkit of learning and strategic/actionable tools applicable directly to business. [[magna-vip-site]]
