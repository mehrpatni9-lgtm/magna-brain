**TL;DR:** Magna's proprietary applied-reflection technique — registered trademark. *"Inking the thinking."* Sits inside the Distillation pillar of [[hyper-learning-framework]]. The premise: thinking that doesn't get inked doesn't compound.

## Why it matters for Magna

Think in Ink ® is Magna's answer to the question *"why is this different from other reflective leadership programs?"* The answer: reflection is operationalised — written, dated, structured — not left as private contemplation. Inked thinking is the substrate the rest of the curriculum builds on.

It also connects to Shailu's broader writing philosophy: *"Reading is for the mind and writing is for the soul."* See [[shailu-books-deep-read]]. Writing is how Shailu thinks. Magna teaches founders to do the same.

## What it actually is (from source)

- A **proprietary framework for applied reflection** — *"inking the thinking."* [[magna-curriculum-r4]]
- Lives inside Pillar 2 (Distillation) of [[hyper-learning-framework]].
- Active reflection turning what was learned into written, durable artefacts the founder can return to.

## Why "ink"

The verb is doing work. To *ink* is to commit. Ink is permanent in a way that dictation, voice notes, or memory are not. The framework isn't called "Write the Thinking" — it's called Ink the Thinking, signalling commitment, not just capture.
