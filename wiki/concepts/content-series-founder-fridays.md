**TL;DR:** Instagram Friday reel or story carousel. Real founder stories at inflection points. Simon Sinek register — human first, lesson second.

## Format

- **Platform:** Instagram reel or carousel
- **Cadence:** Every Friday
- **Voice:** Simon Sinek — start with the human, the emotion before the lesson
- **Structure:** Specific scene (person + moment + decision) → the shift → the lesson
- **Ending:** The lesson is the last line, stated as a transferable truth, not a motivational quote

## Ground rules

- **Always specific.** "$20M company," "three years," "$2M to $18M." Abstract stories don't land.
- **The founder doesn't have to be named.** Many `reference/` examples use "he"/"she" without naming — anonymity is fine.
- **Start with the human, not the company.** Never open with a logo or market.
