**TL;DR:** Instagram Sunday single static. A reflective quote or principle that makes a founder think differently about the week ahead. Daniel Pink register — motivational but grounded.

## Format

- **Platform:** Instagram single static
- **Cadence:** Every Sunday
- **Voice:** Daniel Pink — autonomy, mastery, purpose; motivational but research-grounded
- **Structure:** One line (quote or principle) + 40–70 word caption that reframes it
- **Ending:** A reflective question or a provocation the reader can sit with

## Ground rules

- **Sunday is for reflection, not information.** No stats, no stories, no carousel sprawl.
- **One idea, one image.** No multi-slide.
- **Either a Shailu quote or a Magna principle** — nothing else.
