**TL;DR:** LinkedIn Thursday short text post. A contrarian view on a widely-held business belief. Seth Godin register. Drives the most comments and shares of any series.

## Format

- **Platform:** LinkedIn short text post
- **Cadence:** Every Thursday
- **Author:** [[shailu]]
- **Voice:** Seth Godin — two-sentence observation that rewires how you see the market
- **Structure:**
  1. A common belief stated plainly
  2. The contrarian reframe in the next sentence
  3. One optional supporting line
- **Length:** 20–60 words. **If it's longer than 60 words, it's not a Sharp Take.**

## Ground rules

- **Punchy, not preachy.** No "Here's why I think…" — just state it.
- **Reframes, not denials.** "Hustle culture isn't a work ethic. It's a distraction strategy." — the belief isn't wrong, it's mis-categorised.
- **Ends without a question sometimes.** Sharp Takes can close on the reframe itself when the silence does the work.

## Exemplars

- [[li-sharp-take-001-hustle]] — "Hustle culture isn't a work ethic. It's a distraction strategy."
- [[li-sharp-take-002-pitch-deck]] — "Your pitch deck is not your strategy. It's your strategy for raising money."
- [[li-sharp-take-003-networking]] — "Networking events don't build networks. Shared problems do."
