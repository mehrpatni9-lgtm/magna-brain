**TL;DR:** The culture pillar of [[magna-compass]]. Core teaching: culture isn't what you say. It's what you tolerate.

## Why it matters for Magna

Culture posts on other platforms are mostly slogans. Magna's position: culture is structural — it's the behaviour at the edge that the leader chooses not to name. Every tolerance creates permission. Every permission creates expectation. Culture compounds.
