**TL;DR:** The capital pillar of [[magna-compass]]. Core teaching: capital is a tool, judgment is the multiplier. Revenue is lagging; capital allocation is leading.

## Why it matters for Magna

Most founders optimise for capital access. Magna's position: access is a commodity; allocation is the skill. What founders need is a leading-indicator framework for where capital actually compounds — not a fundraising playbook.
