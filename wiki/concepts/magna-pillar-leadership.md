**TL;DR:** The leadership pillar of [[magna-compass]]. Core teaching: at each phase ($10M → $50M → exit), the business demands a different version of the founder. Becoming that version is the actual work.

## Why it matters for Magna

Most leadership content is about "inspiring your team." Magna's position: the founder's own developmental arc is the actual variable. The business will outlast the version of you that started it — the question is whether you're becoming the person the next phase needs.
