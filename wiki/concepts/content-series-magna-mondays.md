**TL;DR:** Instagram Monday static. One sharp business truth per week. Naval Ravikant register. The post founders screenshot before 9am.

## Why it matters for Magna

`#MagnaMondays` is Magna's recognition weapon — the post that establishes the brand is dense, compressed, and worth saving. It's the opposite of motivational posters. It's the distinction a reader reaches for later in the week when the decision gets hard.

## Format

- **Platform:** Instagram static, 1080×1080
- **Cadence:** Every Monday
- **Voice:** Naval Ravikant — dense, compressed, one big idea, no fluff
- **Structure:** Hook (the screenshot-worthy line) + 60–90 word caption that earns it
- **Ending:** Either a quiet product mention ("That's what Magna is built for") or nothing — never a CTA
