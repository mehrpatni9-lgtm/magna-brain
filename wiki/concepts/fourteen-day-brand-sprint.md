**TL;DR:** The execution path of the masterclass — *"Two weeks. One living asset."* Fourteen sequenced days turn the [[magna-model-five-pillars]] into a published, compounding founder brand, with AI doing the drafting on a **Generate → Expand → Repurpose** loop. "AI drafts · You direct · The asset compounds." [[magna-masterclass-deck-r1]]

## Why it matters for Magna

It's the "fourteen days to the asset" half of the masterclass promise — the doing, not just the model. A ready-made, productizable program the community can be walked through, and a natural lead-in to a Magna cohort or offer.

## Week 1 — foundation + engine

*(Authority · Liking · Commitment)*
Day 1 Niche & Purpose · Day 2 Identity & Visual · Day 3 Strategy & Calendar · Day 4 Signature Story · Day 5 Content & Repurpose · Day 6 Profiles & Hub · Day 7 Series & Lead Magnet.

## Week 2 — distribute, monetize, compound

*(Reciprocity · Social Proof · Scarcity · Unity)*
Day 8 Authority & Search · Day 9 Community & Collab · Day 10 Social & Video · Day 11 Email List · Day 12 Offers & Products · Day 13 Live & Income · Day 14 Measure & Plan 90.

## The AI workflow

1. **Generate** — from niche [X] + audience [Y], draft 14 escalating daily content ideas (intro → offer).
2. **Expand** — take Day [N]'s idea, write the full post in your voice: hook, value body, one CTA.
3. **Repurpose** — turn that post into a 30-sec video script, a 5-slide carousel, and a short email.

## The Standard — three non-negotiables

Consistency (a promise kept on a schedule) · Value (give until uncomfortable) · Voice (AI drafts, you decide). "Perfect is a museum. Living is a market."
