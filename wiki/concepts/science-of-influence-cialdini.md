**TL;DR:** Persuasion is a science, not an art. Robert Cialdini's seven forces — **Reciprocity · Commitment & Consistency · Social Proof · Authority · Liking · Scarcity · Unity** — are the engine under every pillar of the masterclass. The reframe: *"You are not posting. You are pre-suading."* **Pre-suasion** is the privileged moment before the message lands — "your brand is the soil, your content is the seed." [[magna-masterclass-deck-r1]]

## Why it matters for Magna

It's the scientific backbone of [[magna-model-five-pillars]] and gives Magna content a defensible, non-fluffy rationale: every tactic ties to a documented principle of influence. It connects cleanly to [[magna-enables-buying]] (Magna's sales philosophy) and to the brand voice work — persuasion with receipts, not hype.

## The seven principles

1. **Reciprocity** — give first; people feel compelled to give back. *Apply:* lead every launch by giving your best framework away free.
2. **Commitment & Consistency** — small public yeses lead to larger ones. *Apply:* ask for one tiny action, then build toward the offer.
3. **Social Proof** — in doubt, people follow others like them. *Apply:* show testimonials, counts, and logos beside every claim.
4. **Authority** — credentials and proof earn trust. *Apply:* lead with data, results, named credentials.
5. **Liking** — we say yes to people we relate to. *Apply:* show the human behind the brand — flaws, story, values.
6. **Scarcity** — what is rare feels more valuable. *Apply:* cap access, set deadlines, never fake the limit.
7. **Unity** — shared identity turns an audience into a tribe. *Apply:* name your community and speak to a shared mission.

## Pillars meet principles (the bridge)

- Identity + Proof → **Authority**
- Narrative → **Liking**
- System → **Commitment & Consistency**
- Reach + Value → **Reciprocity & Social Proof**
- Revenue + Community → **Scarcity & Unity**
