**TL;DR:** LinkedIn Tuesday long-form text. One operating truth per week, numbered, posted by [[shailu]]. Ray Dalio register. Builds the Magna canon over time.

## Format

- **Platform:** LinkedIn long-form text post
- **Cadence:** Every Tuesday
- **Author:** [[shailu]], as a practitioner with 25 years of scars
- **Voice:** Ray Dalio — numbered principles, radical transparency about failures
- **Structure:**
  1. Title line: `MAGNA PRINCIPLE #<number>: "<the principle, in quotes>"`
  2. 4–8 short paragraphs unpacking the principle with concrete detail
  3. Em-dash break
  4. A closing question inviting a specific confession or story
- **Length:** 150–280 words

## Ground rules

- **Numbered.** Every principle has a number. The canon compounds.
- **Unpacked with depth, not hype.** No exclamation points. No motivational language.
- **Pattern-recognition tone.** "I've seen this a hundred times" energy without claiming authority explicitly.
- **No selling. Ever.** The product sells itself when the thinking is undeniable.
- **Ends with a question** that generates real comments, not performance comments.

- [[li-the-principle-004-negotiation]] — "Never negotiate a deal you're not prepared to walk away from."

## Principle numbering

Principles are numbered sequentially and stored in the order they publish. Gaps are intentional (some numbers are reserved for principles still being unpacked). Next available number: **#012** (assuming #004 and #011 are the only two publicly released so far per `reference/`).
