**TL;DR:** Magna's flagship program name and underlying philosophy — registered trademark. Subtitled *"Applied-MBA in a box."* The phrase that holds the whole education product together: not "an MBA" (gradual, theoretical) but a **quantum** of greatness (transformational, experiential). Maps to the [[quantum-worldview]] applied to founder development.

## Why it matters for Magna

"Quantum of Greatness" is the brand-and-program lock-up that lets Magna avoid being seen as another exec-ed shop. Three things it asserts in one phrase:

1. *Quantum* — transformational, not incremental.
2. *of Greatness* — what's being unlocked is greatness, not generic improvement.
3. *®* — Magna takes its IP seriously enough to register it.

For content, this phrase is highly postable: it carries Shailu's worldview, the program's promise, and the Magna register all at once.

## What it formally is

- Full name: **Magna: Quantum of Greatness ®**.
- Also titled: **Magna Business Administration (MBA) — Quantum of Greatness® Certification Program**.
- Subtitle: *"Applied-MBA in a box — research-backed, action-packed, experiential hyper-learning and engagement framework."*
- Format: 10 weekly remote workshops + 2-day in-person graduation. [[magna-academy]].
- Pedagogy: [[hyper-learning-framework]].
- Leadership development: [[abcd-leadership-framework]].
- Lifecycle frame: [[creation-acceleration-transition]].

## Class 10 callback

The 10th and final class of the curriculum is also titled *"Quantum of Greatness — The Global Entrepreneur"* — explicitly positioned as the capstone where the founder integrates everything they've learned. [[magna-curriculum-r4]]
