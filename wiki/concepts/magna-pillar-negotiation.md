**TL;DR:** The negotiation pillar of [[magna-compass]]. Core teaching: preparation is not knowing your numbers — it's knowing what you'll do if the deal doesn't close.

## Why it matters for Magna

Negotiation is where founders lose the most value they never see. Magna's position: walkaway power is not aggression, it's preparation. The founders who win negotiations are the most *unattached*, not the most forceful.
