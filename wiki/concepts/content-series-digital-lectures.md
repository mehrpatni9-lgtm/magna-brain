**TL;DR:** LinkedIn Saturday bi-weekly 8–10 slide carousel. Full business lecture, free and ungated. "Give the lecture away. Sell the application."

## Format

- **Platform:** LinkedIn document/carousel, 8–10 slides
- **Cadence:** Every other Saturday
- **Author:** [[shailu]]
- **Voice:** Ray Dalio — authoritative, pattern-distilled
- **Structure:**
  1. Title slide
  2. The common trap (1–2 slides)
  3. The shift in thinking (1–2 slides)
  4. The framework (3–4 slides)
  5. The application (1 slide)

## Known lectures (from `reference/`)

1. **Negotiation:** 5 Laws of Strategic Negotiation — "Most founders lose negotiations before they walk into the room."
2. **Sales:** Why Your Sales Process Is Costing You Deals — the product-vs-transformation frame.
3. **Capital:** Capital Allocation: The Skill No One Teaches Founders — leading-indicator framework.
4. **Culture:** How to Build a Culture That Outlasts the Founder — behaviour at the edge.

## Relationship to [[magna-compass]]

Each lecture expands one principle from one pillar into a complete framework. The lecture-to-pillar mapping is:
- Negotiation lecture → [[magna-pillar-negotiation]]
- Sales → a subset of [[magna-pillar-strategy]]
- Capital → [[magna-pillar-capital]]
- Culture → [[magna-pillar-culture]]
- Leadership → [[magna-pillar-leadership]]

## Ground rules

- **Free and ungated.** No lead magnet gates. No email wall. No CTA.
- **One lecture = one topic.** No multi-topic lectures.
- **The application is the sell.** The lecture's only ask is "apply the framework" — implicitly, that's the door into [[quantum-leap]] and [[dubai-roundtables]].
