**TL;DR:** Instagram Wednesday 5–8 slide carousel. Brand-history case studies ending with a Magna framework takeaway. Seth Godin register.

## Why it matters for Magna

The deep-dive that reframes a well-known story (Apple 1997, Patagonia, Dubai) through Magna's worldview. Proves the framework works on famous cases before the reader has to trust it on their own.

## Format

- **Platform:** Instagram carousel, 5–8 slides
- **Cadence:** Every Wednesday
- **Voice:** Seth Godin — provocative but never loud, remarkable thinking about ordinary things
- **Structure:** Hook (surprising factual frame) → story beats across slides → Magna framework takeaway on final slide
- **Ending:** "Swipe to see how" or "Swipe for the Magna takeaway" — never a sale
