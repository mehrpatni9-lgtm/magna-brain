**TL;DR:** The May 2026 plan in one frame. Three projects (digital outreach, physical outreach, website refresh) sit on top of one positioning decision (strategy firm, not classroom) and three execution rails (Founder Fridays, content personalization, prioritized cities). California and Dubai are the geographic priorities. Strategy consulting leads the offer.

## Why it matters for Magna

This is the operational blueprint for May. Every piece of content Mehr produces, every prospect Soman profiles, every feature Anil ships, and every meeting Shailu books in May should be auditable against this concept. If a task doesn't ladder back to one of these priorities, it's drift.

## The three projects

1. **Digital outreach** — 5 companies/week from the 94-person prospect list. May 1 → May 30. Target: 25 companies contacted.
2. **Physical outreach** — Dubai and Chicago office visits. 5 Dubai companies scheduled in May.
3. **Website refresh** — testimonial page updated mid-May with currently engaged contacts. Removed: Martha, Ryan, Sriram. Added: GLC attendees, Gali (Morocco), Marco (Serbia).

## The three execution rails

### Rail 1 — Founder Fridays interview series

Monthly cadence: one founder interview per month → produces multiple `#FounderFridays` posts. Initial candidates: **Sylvie** (first), **Heather Conrad**, **Ani**. Mehr conducts Zoom interviews; Shailu facilitates introductions. See [[content-series-founder-fridays]] for production model.

### Rail 2 — Personalization framework

Every post tags **individual + company + industry + geography + area of interest**. No bulk tagging. Posts must benefit the tagged contact and Magna's outreach simultaneously. See [[personalization-tagging-framework]].

### Rail 3 — Prioritized cities

May focus: **California + Dubai**. Total key cities to be confirmed by Shailu: 10–15. Outreach calendar built city-by-city.

## The three customer buckets

All customers triage into **Economic / Strategy / Capital**. Soman's prioritization task. See [[customer-buckets-economic-strategy-capital]].

## The lead offering

**Strategy consulting** — highest customer appetite of all Magna offerings. Email templates, content, and CTAs lead with this.

## Content production target

5–6 personalized content pieces by end of May. Focused on prioritized cities and community members.
